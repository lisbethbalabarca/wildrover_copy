Welcome to Wild Rover La Paz

The legendary Wild Rover La Paz was founded in 2007 in La Paz, Bolivia. We came across a very run down, old ex presidents house and we transformed it into the best hostel/bar in Bolivia voted by Hostelworld for 4 consecutive years.

Wild Rover also has the highest Irish bar in the world located at over 3600 meters above sea level. Famous for wild parties and great times every night, we are located two blocks from the presidential palace with the perfetc base to explore this amazing city.

Situated in the heart of South America it' a must on the gringo trail, with so many trips and tours available out of La Paz be sure to leave enough time for your stay in the world's highest capital.

As usual Wild Rover does not disappoint with top quality facilities including the comfiest beds in S.A. with real duvets, restaurant, bar, travel agency, free pool table, free internet and wi-fi, 24 hour security guard's with CCTV, free luggage storage and a great party atmosphere every night.

Wild Rover is the perfect place to meet a wide variety of travelers from all over the world.
